U.S. Inflation Data Released: Core CPI Rises 0.3%

The Bureau of Labor Statistics reported today that the Consumer Price Index (CPI) increased 0.3% sequentially, slightly above the consensus estimate of 0.2%. Core CPI, which excludes volatile food and energy prices, also rose 0.3%. Year-over-year inflation stands at 3.2%. The immediate market reaction saw a spike in Treasury yields as investors pared back expectations for near-term rate cuts by the Federal Reserve. Equities, particularly growth and tech sectors, faced downward pressure in early trading following the release.
